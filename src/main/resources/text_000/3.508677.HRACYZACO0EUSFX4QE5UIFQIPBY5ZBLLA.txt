Handheld Basics

* Press any application button to turn on your handheld computer and start a main application.

* Tap New to create a new record.

* Tap a record to edit it.

* Tap Details to access more options or delete the record.

* Tap the pick list in the upper-right corner to categorize records or create a new category. (Not in Date Book.)

* Tap the information ("i") icon in dialog boxes for tips.

* Press the HotSync
 button on the cradle to exchange and update data between your computer and your handheld.
