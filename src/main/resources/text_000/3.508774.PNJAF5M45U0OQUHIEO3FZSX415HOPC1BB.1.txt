
DISCUSSION DOCUMENT FOR THE PURCHASE AND SALE OF NATURAL GAS

September 2001



BUYER: Enron North America Corp (“Enron”)

SELLER:  Kennedy Oil (Kennedy)

CONTRACT
QUANTITY: Dedication of 100% of Kennedy’s coal bed methane production from North, Central and South Kitty fields up to a total of 60,000 MMBtu per day

CONTRACT
TERM: December 1, 2001 – October 31, 2003

DELIVERY
POINT: At the interconnection between the Kennedy wellhead facilities and the Bear Paw Energy pipeline system.

GAS PRICE: For volumes of gas delivered hereunder each day for up to eighty percent (80%) of seller’s First of the Month Scheduled Volume the price will be the following Monthly indexes:
For the first 10,000 MMbtu per day the Contract Price for the applicable Period of Delivery (the “Relevant Period”) shall be the published index specified below:  
Publication: Inside F.E.R.C.’s Gas Market Report
Section: Prices of Spot Gas Delivered to Pipelines
Location: Natural Gas Pipeline Company (Mid-continent zone)
Price: Index minus $.47 less the applicable gathering services fees
Issue: First-of-the-month issue for each month during the Relevant Period.

For all gas volumes above 10,000 MMbtu per day up to 15,000 Mmbtu per day the Contract Price for the applicable Period of Delivery (the “Relevant Period”) shall be:  
Price: The settlement price of the NYMEX Henry Hub gas futures contract for the last trading day of the applicable delivery month, defined as NX1, minus $.62 less the applicable gathering services fees
For all gas above the initial 15,000 MMbtu per day (up to 80% of the First of the Month Scheduled Volume) the Contract Price for the applicable Period of Delivery (the “Relevant Period”) shall be the published index specified below:  
Publication: Inside F.E.R.C.’s Gas Market Report
Section: Prices of Spot Gas Delivered to Pipelines
Location: Colorado Interstate Gas (North System)
Price: Index minus $.03 less the applicable gathering services fees
Issue: First-of-the-month issue for each month during the Relevant Period.
The remaining 20% of the First of the Month Scheduled Volume will be priced as follows*:
Publication: Gas Daily
Section: Daily Price Survey - Rockies
Location: Colorado Interstate Gas (North System)
Price: Midpoint index minus $.03 less the applicable gathering services  fees
Issue: Daily issue for the Relevant Period.

*The Gas Daily pricing is meant to capture the swings in production and operations.
SCHEDULING
And PAYMENTS: 


No later than seven days prior to the first day of each month Seller shall provide to Buyer nomination of the quantities seller expects to make available and schedule for delivery each month during the term hereof and buyer shall confirm the quantities buyer will take to seller no later than three days prior to the first day of such month.  This volume will be the “First of the Month Scheduled Volume”. 
All payments will be made based upon the point of title transfer, which in this case is assumed to be the wellhead.  Enron will manage all aspects of Kennedy’s logistics and scheduling.
